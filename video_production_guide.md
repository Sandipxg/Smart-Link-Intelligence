## üéôÔ∏è Voice Recording Script

### Recording Setup
- **Microphone**: Use quality USB microphone or headset
- **Environment**: Quiet room with minimal echo
- **Software**: Audacity (free) or Adobe Audition
- **Format**: WAV or FLAC for editing, MP3 for final

### Voice Direction
- **Pace**: Conversational, not rushed
- **Tone**: Professional but friendly
- **Emphasis**: Stress key features and benefits
- **Pauses**: Natural breaks between sentences
- **Energy**: Enthusiastic but not over-the-top

### Recording Tips
1. Read through entire script first
2. Record in segments (by scene)
3. Leave 2-3 seconds of silence at start/end
4. Record multiple takes of each segment
5. Stay hydrated and take breaks

## üí° Pro Tips for Success

### Screen Recording
1. **Clean Desktop**: Remove distracting icons and files
2. **Browser Setup**: Use clean browser profile, hide bookmarks
3. **Smooth Movements**: Move cursor slowly and deliberately
4. **Consistent Timing**: Keep similar pace throughout
5. **Multiple Takes**: Record each section 2-3 times

### Voice Recording
1. **Warm Up**: Do vocal exercises before recording
2. **Script Marking**: Mark emphasis and pause points
3. **Consistent Distance**: Stay same distance from microphone
4. **Room Treatment**: Use blankets/pillows to reduce echo
5. **Backup Recordings**: Always record backup takes

### Editing Workflow
1. **Rough Cut First**: Get basic structure before details
2. **Audio First**: Sync and perfect audio before video effects
3. **Save Frequently**: Use auto-save and manual saves
4. **Preview Often**: Check work on different devices
5. **Export Tests**: Do test exports before final version
